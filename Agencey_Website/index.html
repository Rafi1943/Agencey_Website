Ongoing Website Maintenance

Each client that hires you to design a custom website needs additional service at some point. Even if the website is built with a content management system (like WordPress) that allows the client to easily add and manage the content on their own, there will still be some maintenance needed at some point.

Hosting

Your web design and development clients will need website hosting, and this is another service you can offer. Best of all, hosting produces ongoing monthly revenue, and as you add more clients, that hosting revenue will increase.

WordPress Support

WordPress is such an incredibly popular content management system that a large percentage of new websites are being built on the platform. According to statistics, WordPress powers 34% of the internet, and that number is rising.

Logo Design

Every business needs a logo, and many of your web design clients will also be looking for a logo designer. If you build a website for a startup, they’ll probably need a logo. If you’re doing a website redesign for a client with an existing business, this may be a good time for that client to consider updating their brand image with a new logo.

Graphic Design

While logo design is one aspect of graphic design, you could design plenty of other things for your clients, like business cards, brochures and marketing materials, labels, and more.

Ad Design
